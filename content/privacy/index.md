## Minimal Footprint
In the spirit of Zen, we strive for a minimal digital footprint. This platform is designed to be a sanctuary, free from data harvesting or intrusive tracking.

## What we (don't) collect
- **Personal Data**: We do not require account creation, email sign-ups, or any personally identifiable information to participate in global meditations.
- **Cookies**: We only use strictly necessary session cookies to maintain your theme preference and current audio mixer settings.
- **Impact Surveys**: When you participate in and complete a meditation session, any "Impact" survey results are aggregated and anonymized. They are never tied to individual users.

## Collective Data
Anonymous participation metrics are collected only to showcase the power of collective presence on our Impact and Community Map pages.

## Complete Transparency
Our logic is open and our intentions are clear. We exist to facilitate planetary healing, not to build a data business.
